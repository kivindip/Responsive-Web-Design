About Leaf & Latte

A cozy café inspired by nature, designed for calm moments and meaningful pauses.

Our Story

“Leaf & Latte began with a simple belief — that coffee is not just a drink, but a pause in the rush of life. Built around warmth, nature, and simplicity, our café was created to give people a place where time slows down and moments feel lighter.”

Our Vision

“To create a calm and nature-inspired café experience where people reconnect with themselves, find balance in everyday life, and enjoy meaningful moments over a cup of coffee.”

Our Mission

“To create a café space that is deeply connected to nature, offering coffee and comfort in a calm, green-inspired environment.”
